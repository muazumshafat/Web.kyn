Who We Are / Our Mission

KYN is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering Kashmiri youth by providing resources and opportunities in education, community engagement, mental wellness, and leadership. We believe in fostering a supportive environment where young individuals can thrive, develop their skills, and become catalysts for positive change within their communities. Our programs are designed to be inclusive, accessible, and impactful, aiming to build a brighter future for the next generation of Kashmiri leaders.
